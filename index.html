ABOUT The Game

A Masterpiece of Storytelling

The purpose of playing Hay Day is to enjoy a relaxing, open-ended farming and business simulation. Players build and expand a farm by growing crops, raising animals, managing resources, and trading goods to level up and design their ultimate country paradise.

Farm Building & Design: Grow crops, care for animals, and upgrade production buildings (like bakeries and sugar mills) to build your ultimate dream farm. Time & Resource Management: Balance your inventory and production lines to fulfill truck, boat, and town visitor orders.
